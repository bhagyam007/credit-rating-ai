Problem Statement

Traditional Credit Rating is Broken

Legacy systems are slow, opaque, and exclude millions of creditworthy individuals.

⏳

Slow Processing

Manual review processes take days or weeks, delaying loan approvals and frustrating borrowers who need quick decisions.

🔒

Black-Box Decisions

Applicants are rejected without clear reasons. Lack of transparency erodes trust and makes appeals nearly impossible.

📉

Thin-File Exclusion

Students, immigrants, and young adults with limited credit history are unfairly penalized by traditional score-based systems.

⚖️

Inherent Bias

Human judgment in underwriting introduces subconscious bias. Our model uses objective, auditable features to ensure fair outcomes.

Methodology

How CreditAI Works

A four-stage pipeline from raw applicant data to a confident credit decision.

1

Data Collection

Applicant submits financial and demographic inputs via a secure form or API call. Data is validated and sanitized before processing.

2

Feature Engineering

Raw inputs are cleaned, normalized, and transformed into 12 predictive features using domain-specific financial logic.

3

ML Inference

Our trained XGBoost ensemble model scores the application in under 300ms, producing a credit score between 300–900.

4

Explainable Output

SHAP values explain exactly which factors drove the score — providing full transparency for both lenders and applicants.

Model Architecture

Under the Hood

XGBoost ensemble with SHAP-based explainability.

Architecture Details

  • 500 gradient-boosted decision trees
  • 5-fold stratified cross-validation
  • SMOTE oversampling for class imbalance
  • Hyperparameter tuning via Optuna (200 trials)
  • SHAP TreeExplainer for local & global explanations
  • Deployed as REST API with FastAPI + Docker
